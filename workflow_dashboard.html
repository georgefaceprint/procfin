Model Architecture

Closed-Loop Managed Procurement

This model is also known as Reverse Factoring with Managed Disbursement or Arbitraged Purchase Order Financing. By procuring goods directly from prequalified suppliers on behalf of Jane, ProcFin fully mitigates cash diversion risk while capturing arbitrage spread at source.

Supplier A delivers products directly to Jane's site to fulfill the tender specifications.
