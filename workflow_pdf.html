System Architecture Overview

ProcFin operates as a Closed-Loop Supply Chain Finance & Managed Procurement Platform. Instead of disbursing cash directly to SMEs (eliminating cash diversion risk), the platform manages procurement directly. By matching the SME's tender specifications with category-specific accredited suppliers and selecting the lowest competitive bid, the platform secures wholesale supply margins while granting the SME a full-value credit facility.
